Damage Leapers

Trial overview & tips

Objective

Eliminate leapers efficiently while avoiding damage. Focus on positioning and ammo conservation.

Tips

  • Map: Dam Battlegrounds
  • Lure leaper by hit it from a distance
  • Play in group and split to detect freshly spawn leaper

Loadout

Heavy armor penetration guns, fire granades, hullcracker, adrenaline shot
